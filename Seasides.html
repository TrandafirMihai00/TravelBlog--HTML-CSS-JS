Anse Source D’Argent in L’Union Estate, Seychelles

Popular for its pristine and sandy white beaches, Seychelles makes for an incredible tropical escapade. Anse Source D Argent is a cherry on the top! This white sand beach has been featured in many adverts, movies, and TV shows. Book a resort nearby for an unforgettable Seychelles tour that allows a lot of beach time and innumerable scenic sights.

Famous For: Mesmerizing natural surroundings and scenic views.
Best time to visit: April to May and October to November
Things to do at Anse Source D Argent: Snorkeling, sea-turtle spotting, and swimming

Navagio Beach in Zakynthos, Greece

One of the most beautiful white sand beaches in the world, Navagio Beach in Greece is an iconic vacation spot. Also known as Shipwreck Beach, it has MV Panagiotis (Freightliner wreck) as a prime attraction. With such captivating beauty, it is certainly among the most magnificent places to visit in Greece.

Famous For: inspiring surroundings.
Best time to visit: March to May
Things to do at Navagio Beach: Scuba diving, snorkeling, Base jumping, and kayaking

Boulders Beach in Cape Town, South Africa

Boulders Beach or Boulders Bay is among the best white sand beaches in the world. And its beauty is further accentuated by the African penguins colony which has been here since 1982. The beach also is a part of Table Mountain National Park, a top tourist attraction of South Africa. This is probably one of the best white sand beaches in the world, 2021.

Famous For: Home to African penguins.
Best time to visit: Anytime
Things to do at Boulders Beach: Interacting with the penguins interaction, swimming, and photography

Kaunaoa Bay in Hawaii, USA

Who doesn’t dream of a Hawaiian vacation? And why not, after all Hawaii is home to some of the best white sand beaches in the world. Kauna’oa Bay, crescent-shaped beach, is the most popular of them all. The luxurious Westin Mauna Kea Beach Hotel is located right on the beach and attracts beach lovers and luxury seekers from all over the world.

Famous For: Encompasses an array of luxury facilities.
Best time to visit: April to October
Things to do at Kauna’oa Bay: Snorkeling, Manta Ray spotting, sunbathing, and photography

Tulum Beach, Mexico

With the Mayan ruins perched on a cliff right beside it, Tulum Beach is among the most beautiful white sand beaches in the world. Though the picturesque landscape will make your heart skip a beat, there are beach clubs and resorts that ensure you have a gala time.

Famous For: Glistening blue waters and spotless beach.
Best time to visit: December to April
Things to do at Tulum Beach: Visit the Mayan ruins, snorkeling, swimming, and go for long strolls
